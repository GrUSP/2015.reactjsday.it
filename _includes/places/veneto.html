By car

From A4 Verona Sud Exit: follow the direction to Fiera - Verona Centro. Drive along Viale delle Nazioni. Continue along Viale del Lavoro until you reach the intersection with Viale dell'Agricultura. Turn right and, reached the end of the street, turn left. Drive straight along Via Santa Teresa to the entrance of the compound on the left.

By train

From Verona Porta Nuova station (Frecciargento): take bus number 51 at platform "B" direction "SACRA FAMIGLIA" (here is the time schedule for bus #51). The run will be from “STAZIONE PORTA NUOVA” to “VIA S. TERESA 51“, which is really near the venue (go back for about 100 meters).

You can also get to the venue by taxi. They are near the exit, on the right.

By plane

From Verona Villafranca Airport: Take the Tangenziale following Verona direction. Take the junction to Verona Centro. At the end of the Tangenziale turn right into Via Luciano dal Cero. At the second traffic light drive straight and then turn right into Viale Piave. Stay on the third lane (Modena - Ferrara - P Fiera). The entrance of the venue is just at the end of the underpass on the right.
